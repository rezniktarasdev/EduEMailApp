    You can also organize any projects you’re working on through boards, which you'll be able to customize either solo or with others (ideal for delegating tasks for a group assignment). There is an option to create separate lists for tasks on your to-do list, tasks you’re doing now, and tasks you’ve completed.

  • All in One Notes

    Providing one more option for keeping your thoughts as well as your assignments organized, EduEMailApps lets you organize your thoughts by creating mind maps, which can be seamlessly synchronized to your personal cloud and shared with others. You can also enhance your mind maps with photos, videos and voice memos, and easily customize the appearance and select different layouts to suit you.

  • Experienced Traveller

    There is one more superb addition - EduEMailApps can collate smart information from those other services into a little travel pack. For example, based on flight information you've saved in Calendar, EduEMailApps can guess where you're going and when. Based on stars you've added to your personal TravelMap, it can help you focus on sites you want to visit while there. All the photos, videos, made during the travel or published on Instagram, visited places, and saved links will be put into separate sections.
